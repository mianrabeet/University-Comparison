What "not restricted" actually means here

None of these six appear on the publicly reported pause list (Chester, Wolverhampton, East London, Sunderland, Coventry, Hertfordshire, Oxford Brookes, Glasgow Caledonian, BPP, Derby). That is different from a guarantee — confirm directly with each admissions office before paying any deposit, since:

  • UKVI's new Red-Amber-Green compliance system rates universities on their own sponsor-license anniversary date, not all at once — a university's rating can change mid-cycle.
  • Pakistani visa refusal rates remain well above the 5% sponsor threshold nationally, so any university can announce a pause with little notice.
  • Visa processing delays — not CAS pause — have been the more common problem reported for Pakistani applicants in this cycle.
